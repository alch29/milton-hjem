User Guide

User guide for Milton Hjem.

Getting Started

To get started, visit the app via this link: url placeholder. When you arrive at the login page, log in with the email and password that have been provided to you.

For Construction Clients

This section is specifically aimed at construction clients, but is also relevant for construction consultants.

Navigating the App

You can navigate between the different pages of the app by clicking the icons in the navigation bar at the bottom of the screen.

Viewing the Construction Progress

After logging in, you will be directed to the homepage, where you can see how far along the construction is, along with a preview of a timeline showing important dates and meetings that will take place during the construction process. The timeline can be expanded to a full view, where you can also toggle between viewing it in timeline format or as a classic calendar.

The Timeline

The events in the timeline are colour-coded according to their type.

  • White: Indicates the main focus of the day, or that a new phase of the construction process has begun.
  • Yellow: Indicates that a meeting between you and the construction consultant will take place.
  • Blue: The next upcoming event or meeting.
  • Dark blue: The day construction is complete and you receive the keys to your new home.
  • Greyed out: The event or meeting has already passed.

Viewing Images and Documents

All images and documents sent to you by the construction consultant will be organised in folders, accessible by clicking the "Images" or "Documents" icons in the navigation bar. Inside an image folder, images are organised in batches and can be viewed by clicking on a specific batch. You can search for a specific batch using the search bar or the sorting options at the top of the page. The same applies when searching for specific documents in a document folder.

Messaging the Consultant

On the messages page, you can message your construction consultant directly by clicking on their card. Before reaching out, it may be worth browsing the Q&A section, as your question may already be answered there.

Viewing Contact Information

Clicking the "More" icon will take you to a page where you can view your own settings and the contact information of your consultant.

For Construction Consultants

This section covers features that are only relevant for consultants.

Browsing Projects

After logging in, you will be directed to a page displaying all your current projects. You can search for a specific project using the search bar at the top of the page, and select a project by clicking on it.

Uploading Documents and Images

To upload documents or images for a specific client, select the client and then click the upload icon in the navigation bar. You will be prompted to choose between uploading images or documents, after which an upload window will open. Click the upload field and select the file or files you wish to upload. When uploading images, the title you enter will become the name of the image batch. Before confirming the upload, select a category for the file or files — this will also determine which folder they are organised into.
